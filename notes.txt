Mapping of Datasets to Services
etsr_events.csv:

Service: Catalog Service
Database: catalog_db
Purpose: Stores event details.
etsr_venues.csv:

Service: Catalog Service
Database: catalog_db
Purpose: Stores venue details.
etsr_users.csv:

Service: User Service
Database: user_db
Purpose: Stores user profiles and credentials.
etsr_seats.csv:

Service: Seating Service
Database: seating_db
Purpose: Stores seat inventory and pricing.
etsr_orders.csv:

Service: Order Service
Database: order_db
Purpose: Stores order details and statuses.
etsr_tickets.csv:

Service: Order Service
Database: order_db
Purpose: Stores ticket details linked to orders and seats.
etsr_payments.csv:

Service: Payment Service
Database: payment_db
Purpose: Stores payment transactions and statuses.
